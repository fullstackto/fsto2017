What we cover

Client Side Code & Architecture

Our client side code stream is where you will expereince hands on training with technologies primarily used on the front-end such as React.js, Ember.js, Native JavaScript, and more. These are our practical front-end sessions.

Our client side architecture stream is where you will learn about other organiziations architecture challenges and how they solved them, these are our more therotical front-end coding sessions.

Server Side Code & Architecture

Our server side code stream is where you will learn new techniques with technologies primarily used on the back-end such as Java, Ruby, Spring Boot, and more. These are our practical back-end coding sessions.

Our server side architecture stream is where you will learn about other organiziations delivery and data architectures. Topics like scaling, performance, devops, content delivery fall into these case-study like sessions.

User Experience

We woudln't be full stack without also including designers in our event. Bringing in designers also provides amazing networking oportunities.

Our ux stream is where you will find talks that will help designers and developers alike create a better web, topics like user flows, chatbot and vr expereinces, user offboarding, making the user feel secure and so much more are in these case-study type sessions.

IoT, Soft Skills, and New Technology

We also know that coding or designing all day can be exhausting so we've sprinkled in a few sessions to help you think differently in your everyday process.

Internet of Things talks will show you how you can "hack" the world, and important factors in doing so. Soft Skills will help be a better leader. Finally the New Technology stream is where you can be inspired by technology gaining momentem!

The #fstoco experience

Diversity & Inclusion

#fstoco is organized by a diverse group of individuals, our Executive Director identifies as a genderqueer homosexual, our board is comprised of people of colour, and our volunteers come from all walks of life. However, we don't stop there! We pay close attention to our speaker lineup to ensure differnt points of views from differnt types of people are presented, especially women, we make sure that our event is a safe space for learning including gender non-binary washrooms and an enforeced code of conduct. We also take great pride in attracting attendees from all from all around the world. Toronto is diverse and so should it's leading tech event!

Great Content

Our event planning process starts with great quality content, we carefully select technologies, tool, and methods to cover in our event, lay them out in the schedule so you get exposed to more than just your specialization, then find an amazing speaker to talk in that spot. This is a lot differnt than the traditional events that just pick from a list of submited sessions, we seek out great content and great speakers to present it!

Networking

We know that not all out you are extroverts and that these large social settings can make it hard to feel included and strike up a conversation. We design our expereince around breaking down barriers. Our event space is barrier free to ease of access for all, we offer you a space on your name tag to put your passion so someone else can make that first intro, and we've but games, lego, and other ice breakers around the venue so you can come together over a common task and feel less like you're being intterogated.
