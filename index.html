Our Programs

Advanced Workshops

Plus Equal Tech is our advanced workshop program. These are one day events held on Saturday's in downtown Toronto. They focus on providing an introduction to an advanced topic with a talk by a company using the technology, followed by two other related talks, then a deep dive workshop all afternoon where attendees will create something with that advanced tech.

Monthly Meetups

Our monthly meetups consit of one or two talks often focused on telling a story about a technical journey, such as refactoring launching a new platform, challenges and wins with new or old technology and much more. These are after work events to keep you up to date with what's going on in the development scene in Toronto.

Yearly Conference

Every year we gather to bring together front-end, back-end, and dev-ops developers to meet each other in an inclusive atmosphere for learning new technolgies, storytelling, and of course friendship building level networking. The content and schedule are designed to get you out of your speciality and thinking about the other aspects of development.

Socials

We're happy to be apart of the TechMasters Social group for technologies. This is a slack group where professionals come for help, support, and to all around chat. There are also offline socials where we get together and discuss in person the latest and greatest and of course painful tech news. These happen in a bar in downtown toronto and are a great way to network outside of our learning based events.
